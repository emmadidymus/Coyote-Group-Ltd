OUR MANAGEMENT TEAM

Meet the visionary minds leading Coyote Group Ltd

CPA(T) Augustus Machumi

Executive Director

CPA (T) Augustus Machumi is a seasoned finance expert, agripreneur, and visionary leader recognized for developing and executing projects aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), with a strong focus on creating real impact in people’s lives.

With over 15 years of experience, Augustus has initiated and managed a variety of business ventures, including - beekeeping, pig and poultry farming, and cashew plantations.

In addition to his professional pursuits, Augustus is deeply passionate about empowering youth and women. He mentors skilled young people, guiding them to grow professionally while upholding African cultural values and social norms.

He is the co-founder of Coyote Group Limited and the creative force behind the Crown Cashewnut brand, which specializes in processing and marketing both white and roasted cashew nuts. His long-term vision is to see African-grown foods fully processed and packaged on the continent before being exported to global markets.

Mr. Tumaini .J. Makwasa

Managing Director

Born and raised in Karagwe, a small suburb of Kagera / Tanzania; Makwasa graduated from College of Business Administration with a Diploma in Procurement and Supply Management.

Makwasa has been in the business field for over 10 years. He has worked in flight and forwarding business, fumigation of crops, research activities with different companies, general products supply, and contracts management in business contracts.
