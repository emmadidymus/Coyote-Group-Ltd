About Us

Our story began in 2017. Back then we were retailers of food products in Dar es Salaam City as sole proprietors. We started farming with a few acres in order to satisfy the emerging food market in Tanzania. Today, we are privileged to connect with thousands of customers every day in our roasted and packed cashew nut, unroasted cashew nut, sesame, and coffee markets. We have a range of modern cashew farms practicing organic farming methodologies.

Our Mission

To produce and supply high standardized crops and cereals products which meet world class standards.

Our Vision

To be among leading companies specializing in organic products and environmental services in the entire agricultural value chain.
