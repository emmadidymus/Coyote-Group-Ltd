Our Story.

Organic Roots. Global Reach.

From humble beginnings in 2017 as food retailers in Dar es Salaam, we've grown into a trusted supplier of high-quality agricultural products across Tanzania and beyond. Today, COYOTE GROUP LIMITED (CGL) connects thousands of customers daily through our organically farmed cashew nuts, sesame, and coffee—carefully harvested and processed to meet global standards.
